Banana Milkshake Recipe

A creamy, sweet, and healthy banana milkshake perfect for breakfast or a refreshing snack.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: No cooking required

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or honey
  • 4-5 ice cubes
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)
  • Chopped nuts for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1.

Blend: Add bananas, milk, sugar/honey, ice cubes, and cardamom powder to a blender.

2.

Process: Blend until smooth and creamy.

3.

Serve: Pour into glasses and garnish with chopped nuts if desired.

Serving Tips

  • Use frozen bananas for an extra thick shake.
  • Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a richer taste.
  • Sprinkle cinnamon or cocoa powder on top for flavor twist.
  • Best served immediately for freshness and texture.
