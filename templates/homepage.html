payesh

"Payesh, a beloved Bengali dessert, is a rich and creamy rice pudding made with milk, sugar, and flavored with cardamom, often served during celebrations like birthdays and festivals in Bangladesh."

kulfi

"Kulfi, a traditional frozen dessert in Bangladesh, is a dense and creamy treat made from slow-cooked milk, sugar, and nuts, often enjoyed during summer."

samossa

"Samossa, a popular snack in Bangladesh, is a crispy, deep-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes, meat, or vegetables, often enjoyed with tea."

faluda

"Faluda, a popular dessert in Bangladesh, is a refreshing sweet treat made with rose syrup, vermicelli, basil seeds, and milk, often topped with ice cream for an extra indulgence."

lassi

"Lassi, a refreshing drink in Bangladesh, is made from yogurt blended with water, sugar, or salt, and is especially popular during the hot summer months."

masala tea

"Masala tea, loved in Bangladesh, is a spiced milk tea brewed with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, offering a warm and flavorful experience."
