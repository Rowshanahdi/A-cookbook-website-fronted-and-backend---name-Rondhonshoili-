Simple Beef Biryani Recipe

A slow-cooked beef biryani stew rich with spices and flavor.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (small pieces)
  • 2 cups-Basmati rice
  • 2 large Onion(sliced)
  • 1-Tomato (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Biryani masala
  • 2 Green chili(slit)
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • Salt to taste & water as needed
  • Coriander & mint leaves-for garnish

Instructions

1.

Cook Beef: Fry onions in oil, add ginger-garlic paste,tomato,yogurt biryani masala,beef.Cook until beef is soft.

2.

Cook Rice: Boil rice till 70% done.

3.

Layer: In a pot,layer beef masala,rice,fried onions coriander,mint and green chilies.

4.

Add Aroma: Add saffron milk or kewra/rose water.

5.

Dum Cooking: Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 15-20 mins.

Cooking Tips

  • Marinate beef for 1 hour with yogurt and spices for better flavor .
  • Use a pressure cooker for faster,tender meat.
  • Don't overcook rice;it should be firm.
  • Add rose/kewra water or a pinch of saffron for extra aroma.
  • Let biryani rest for meat for 10 minutes after cooking before serving.
